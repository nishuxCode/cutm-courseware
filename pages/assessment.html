Assessment Structure

CUTM follows a continuous evaluation system that focuses on knowledge, skills, and practical learning outcomes.

Continuous Assessment (CA)

Includes quizzes, assignments, class participation, and internal evaluations conducted throughout the semester.

40% Weightage

Mid-Semester Examination

Evaluates conceptual understanding and analytical skills through written and practical assessments.

20% Weightage

End-Semester Examination

Comprehensive evaluation covering the entire syllabus of the course.

30% Weightage

Project & Practical

Hands-on projects, lab work, and real-world problem solving to assess practical competence.

10% Weightage
